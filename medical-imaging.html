Why Thermal Imaging?

Possibilities for Your Health

While our "Standard of Care" uses imaging systems — Angiograms, MRI, Cat Scans, Ultrasound, and Mammograms — that lack the ability to identify pre-cursors to disease, infrared thermal imaging detects the most fundamental factors, including:

  • Chronic (prolonged) inflammation
  • Lymphatic congestion
  • Estrogen dominance

Be proactive with your health. The body is fearfully and wonderfully made and when operating properly can heal itself in so many ways. Find out where the issues are early so you have the highest opportunity for good health. Be the BEST YOU that YOU can be.

Breast Screening

Be proactive by screening for inflammation, lymphatic congestion, and hormonal imbalances. Very early detection saves lives AND breasts.

  • Tumors of the breast
  • Inflammatory conditions of the breast and chest
  • Lymph congestion in the breast and chest

Half Body Screening

From the top of the head to just above the pubic bone — includes all major organs, lymphatics, breasts, periodontal issues, Carotid artery, and coronary concerns.

  • Full cranial and thyroid (includes Carotid Artery)
  • Periodontal inflammation
  • Full breast scan and lymph node activity
  • Full back (upper & lower)
  • Stomach, intestines, kidneys, colon, ovaries, uterus

Full Body Screening

From the top of the head to the bottom of the feet — everything in the Half Body Scan plus legs, lymphatics in the upper thighs, phlebitis, varicose veins, and more.

  • Complete top-to-toe thermal map
  • Varicosity and phlebitis detection
  • Lower extremity lymphatic review

Sports Screening

Locate neuromusculoskeletal injuries, back pain, joint pain, and stress fractures for optimal rehabilitation planning.

  • Muscle and nerve injury mapping
  • Joint inflammation assessment
  • Return-to-competition prognosis
