Medical Imaging

Preparation Protocols

The following applies to all Women's Health Scans, Men's Health Scans, and Breast Studies. Each time window is important — please follow all applicable steps.

24 Hours Prior

Women's Health, Men's Health & Breast Studies

  1. No shaving
  2. No alcohol
12 Hours Prior

All Appointments

  1. No sunbathing or sunburn exposure
  2. No exercising or sauna use
2 Hours Prior

All Appointments

  1. Do not apply any lotions, creams, deodorants, or powders
  2. No eating food
  3. Do not eat or drink any caffeine products
  4. No nicotine products
  5. Avoid taking any medications until after the exam
  6. Avoid any treatments that day (chiropractic, ultrasound, massage, etc.)
  7. No mints or gum chewing
  8. Wear loose fitting clothing to your appointment
  9. Women: remove your bra 30 minutes prior to your appointment
