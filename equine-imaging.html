Why Thermal Imaging?

Physiological Imaging for Your Horse

Thermal imaging is being recognized in the horse world as an effective diagnostic modality. It helps locate areas of inflammation BEFORE they become bigger problems — assisting saddle fit, helping vets find mystery lameness, monitoring hoof health, and tracking healing.

Locate a problem that is stressed BEFORE the affected tissue becomes damaged, and find the CAUSE by utilizing infrared's physiological imaging — much different than anatomical imaging such as X-ray.

All of this with NO radiation, no poking or prodding, no compression — in the comfort of your horse's OWN barn.
